The Challenge

The problem I solve: external growth gaps.

Most growth companies already have smart people, a real product, a clear internal belief, and plenty of activity in motion.

But growth can still stall when the outside market is not responding the way the company expects.

That is the gap I focus on.

I help identify where external momentum may be breaking down, in positioning, partner strategy, credibility, outreach angles, market perception, or the way the company is showing up to prospects, investors, advisors, and strategic partners.

The goal is not to tell your team what they already know. The goal is to surface what may be missing from the outside view, so the next business development move is clearer, sharper, and more likely to create opportunity.

Common signs of the gap:

  • Partnership conversations do not move forward.
  • Investor or advisor interest stalls.
  • Sales conversations create activity but not traction.
  • Announcements generate visibility but not credibility.
  • The company has internal confidence, but the market is not converting that confidence into opportunity.

Diagnostic Tool

Signal Shift is one tool I use to bridge the unknown.

Signal Shift shows whether a company's message, announcement, or external visibility is actually creating credibility, authority, and momentum, or whether the market is interpreting it differently than expected.

It supports my fractional BD and strategic partnership work by helping companies understand what the market is actually hearing before they make the next move. Signal Shift is not the whole business. It is one diagnostic layer I use to help companies see where momentum is forming, where credibility is weak, and which conversations are worth pursuing next.
